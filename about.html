About the Club

A bunch of mates on bikes, riding the west.

Who We Are

Born in the West

Westies CC started the way most good things do — a few people who love cycling and live on the same side of town. No grand plan, no club constitution, just a shared appreciation for early mornings, good legs, and the long flat stretches of the M7 cycle path.

We're based in the western suburbs of Sydney, and that geography shapes everything about us. The M7 is our home route — a car-free corridor that runs alongside the motorway and gives us kilometres of sealed path to train, socialise, and explore on two wheels.

We're an informal crew. There's no membership fee, no grading system, and no expectation that you arrive with a carbon race bike and a power meter. Bring yourself, bring your bike, and you're in.

How We Ride

The Westies Way

No Drop Policy

We ride as a group and finish as a group. If you're struggling, we slow down. Simple as that.

Safety First

Helmets are non-negotiable. We call hazards, signal turns, and look out for each other on every ride.

All Abilities

Whether you're new to group riding or have thousands of kilometres in your legs, you'll find your pace with us.

Community

The coffee stop matters as much as the ride. Good people make good rides, and we've got plenty of both.
